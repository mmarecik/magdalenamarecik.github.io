MY PORTFOLIO

Hello! Thank you for visiting my portfolio. My name is Magda and I'm dedicated Data Scientist with a passion for solving complex data problems. The projects below covers multiple tasks from the field of quantitative analysis and machine learning. Beyond confirming my technical skills, the portfolio offers insights into my development as a data scientist, showcasing projects related to different problems and solved with various approaches. I hope you enjoy exploring it.

Project 1

Comprehensive analysis of travel packages offered on the website of Rainbow Tour travel agency. Project includes using web-scraping to gather data from the company's website, dealing with unbalanced dataset and building multiple classifiers for assessing popularity of each trip among the customers. Ultimate goal is to find significant factors encouraging people to purchase a travel packages.

Project 2

Prediction of the daily movements of the stock open prices of sample commodity with use of OHLC dataset. To supply models with additional information, technical idices were calculated and included during training.

Project 3

Comprehensive analysis of USA transportation infrastructure in R including building ranking with linear ordering methods, clustering and PCA.

Project 4

Monitoring covariance rate of daily returns with use of Pearson index and EWMA & GARCH(1,1) models. Measureing risk of multiple assets portfolio using model-building approach. Code in R.

Project 5

Applying different Statistical Learning methods in a task of detecting people who are keen to buy a travel insurance during introductory offering. Proposed solutions are k-Nearest Neighbours, Naive Bayes Classifier, Logistic Regression and Classification Tree.

Project 6

Custom R package for preliminary data preprocessing. Supports operations on the data such as standarization, missing values imputation and feature encoding.

Project 7

Comprehensive analysis of the hospital data concerning the health and environmental factors of its patients. Identyfication of factors increasing the risk of illness.

Project 8

Analysis of autocorrelation and spectral analysis of Alphabet Inc. stock 5-minute returns. Estimating power spectral density using a periodogram and applying filters to extract low and high frequencies from the series. (Text in Polish)

Project 9

Interactive price scraper that sends notification when product gets cheaper. Project implemented in python using Beautiful Soup package.
